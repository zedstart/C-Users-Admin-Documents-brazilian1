    Why Choose Brazilian Explorers?

    Unmatched Experience and Passion for Brazil’s Biodiversity

    Locally Owned and Operated

    We are a Brazilian company, deeply rooted in the country’s wilderness. Based in Manaus, Amazonas, our team lives and breathes the Brazilian biomes, offering tours with local guides who have strong connections within the ranching communities and a profound understanding of Brazilian culture.

    Over 30 Years of Expertise

    Our extensive experience in guiding tours across Brazil’s diverse ecosystems ensures we know the best spots for wildlife sightings and plant life. We understand animal behavior during different seasons, making our expeditions unique and likely to encounter few other travelers. Our guides are knowledgeable about the entire Brazilian wilderness, not just isolated areas.

    ceptional Local Guides

    Our tours are led by skilled guides with deep knowledge of Brazil’s wildlife and birds. Their mission is to make you feel part of this extraordinary ecosystem, exploring safely and responsibly.

    Custom and Private Tours

    Tailor-made experiences are our specialty. We work with you to create your perfect trip, offering complete freedom to choose what you want to see, where you want to go, and when. Customize our itineraries for couples, friends, or families, adjusting elements to suit your interests.

    Small Group Sizes

    To enhance wildlife viewing, personalized service, and minimal environmental impact, we keep our groups small, limited to just 8 guests. This ensures more time with your guide, enriching your experience.

    Unique Lodges and Camps

    Our accommodations range from rustic to comfortable, integrated seamlessly with the landscape. We carefully select lodges based on seasonal changes in the Pantanal and Amazon. Stay in the best lodges, camps, and family-run, sustainably built accommodations.

    Exceptional Value

    Our tours offer quality services at the best prices, with numerous inclusions like extra meals, flexibility, hikes, adventures, and more time in prime locations. No hidden costs, no extra charges for solo travelers, and plenty of ‘extra touches’ make our Brazil Safari exceptional value for money.

    Affordable Pricing

    We believe in providing high-quality wildlife safaris and nature tours at reasonable prices. As an owner-operated business, we keep overheads low, passing savings directly to you. Compare our prices, and you’ll find us significantly more affordable.

    Commitment to Sustainable Tourism

    Responsible travel is core to our tours. We support ecosystem preservation projects and local community initiatives, ensuring that your travel with us supports sustainable and ethical tourism.
